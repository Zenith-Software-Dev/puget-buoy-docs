Overview

The view includes a blue rectangle that fills the whole screen and ignores the safe area, a green circle, and a white checkmark. When the view appears, the circle and checkmark animate using interpolatingSpring() and easeInOut() transitions, respectively. The “Upload Complete” message is displayed when the animation is finished.
